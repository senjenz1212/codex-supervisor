# PRD Grill Findings

## Finding 1: Provenance Must Be Reported At The Public Report Boundary

Status: resolved.

The useful seam is the paired report, not individual reviewer adapters, because roster-selection mistakes happen when aggregate reports are interpreted. The PRD keeps the first proof at `run_paired_acceptance_pilot` and mirrors the same block into the bounded runner.

## Finding 2: Family Diversity And Tool-Backed Evidence Are Different Claims

Status: resolved.

The PRD separates provider family, tool access, assurance grade, and command-evidence status. A text-only cross-family reviewer can be recorded as useful diversity without being mislabeled as execution-grounded.

## Finding 3: Generator Family May Be Unknown On Fixtures

Status: resolved.

The PRD does not require invented fixture generator family. It only emits same-family warnings when produced baseline metadata records a generator family.

## Finding 4: Roster Selection Must Stay Blocked

Status: resolved.

The PRD preserves fixture-only blocking and adds provenance/disjointness requirements to the guard rather than turning these diagnostics into selection authority.
